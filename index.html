About

This project came about because I really needed to figure out what the temperature was in my apartment, and not just at any given time, but over the course of the day. My building's thermostat can be programmed to adjust itself at certain times, but it's tricky to figure out what temperature and at what times the thermostat should beprogrammed for. This project allows me to figure out if there are any times of the day that the thermostat should be reprogrammed.

There are many different ways of recording temperatures over time. There are even a plethora of ways to publish those readings the web right out of the box. As a vacation project I wanted to build something that allowed me to use a bunch of different tools to accomplish the same thing.
